User Story

    As a passenger,

    I want to pay the fare with credit card.

    So that I can reduce direct contact with the driver, reduce the hassle of paying cash, and speed up pick-up and drop-off times.

Acceptance Criteria 1

    Given:
        User books a trip and navigates to the billing page.

    When:
        User chooses to pay by credit card.

    Then:
        Display credit card payment information fields for users to fill in.

Acceptance Criteria 2

    Given:
        User chooses to pay by credit card in the billing page.

    When:
        User fills in all required credit card information fields.

    Then:
        The trip is confirmed and the user can start enjoying it.
