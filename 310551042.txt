User Story

    As a passenger

    I want to share my live location

    So that my parents or friends could know where I am.

Acceptance Criteria 1

    Given: 
        User open Uber App's driver detail

    When:
        User click "Share Status", chooses parents or friends from contact list and select communication app
    
    Then:
        User's live location details are shared with parents or friends

Acceptance Criteria 2

    Given: 
        Parents or Friends open the communication app

    When:
        Parents or Friends receive a text with a link and open it

    Then:
        Smartphone displays user's driver name, vehicle information, and map location in real time
